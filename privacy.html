Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. Introduction

POPPIN ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Garnet Grid Consulting LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our nightlife platform, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Platform"). By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Full name, username, and email address
  • Date of birth (to verify you are 21+ for alcohol-related content)
  • Profile photo and bio
  • Phone number (optional, for account recovery)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (browser type, OS, device ID)
  • IP address and approximate geolocation
  • Pages visited, features used, and interaction timestamps
  • Check-in history and bar visit patterns
  • Search queries and filter preferences

2.3 User-Generated Content

  • Comments, chat messages, and direct messages
  • Bar ratings and votes
  • Event RSVPs and reviews
  • Profile activity and social interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To operate the Platform, process check-ins, deliver real-time features, and personalize your experience
  • Communication: To send push notifications, platform updates, and promotional content (with opt-out options)
  • Safety & Security: To detect fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, and protect our community
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns, improve features, and generate aggregate insights
  • Advertising: To serve relevant promoted bar listings and event ads (we do not sell your personal data to third parties)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Partner Venues: Anonymized and aggregate check-in and voting data with bar owners who subscribe to our platform
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, payment processing, and communication
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We never sell your personal data to advertisers or third-party data brokers.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted storage for passwords and sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and logging for administrative operations
  • Automated threat detection and incident response procedures

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications and certain data processing
  • Do Not Sell: California residents may request we not sell their personal information (we don't sell personal data)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@garnetgrid.com.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session and login state
  • Remember your preferences and settings

  • Deliver relevant content and promotions

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect Platform functionality.

POPPIN is intended for users aged 21 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 21. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.
